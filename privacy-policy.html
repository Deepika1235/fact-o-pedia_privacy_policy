Information Collection and Use

At Fact-O-Pedia, your privacy is our priority. To provide a comprehensive fact-checking and information service, our app requires access to your device's camera, microphone, and photo gallery for features like Optical Character Recognition (OCR) and voice search. We also aggregate news articles from reputable sources such as The Times of India to provide contextual support for our fact-checks. All data is collected only with your explicit consent and is handled securely in accordance with applicable privacy laws and regulations.
